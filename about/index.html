About

DSRG is a Distributed Systems Reading Group at MIT. We meet once every other week in Stata (room is announced on the mailing list) to discuss distributed systems research papers, and cover papers from conferences like SOSP, OSDI, PODC, VLDB, and SIGMOD. We try to have a healthy mix of current systems papers and older seminal papers.
